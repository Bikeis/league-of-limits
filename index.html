How to Play

Solve calculus questions, outscore your rivals, and stay in the game.

Your Goal

Reach 15 CP, or be the last player standing, to win the match.

Setup: Shuffle 10 question cards and 12 action cards together. Everyone starts with three cards, then rolls two dice. Lowest total goes first.
